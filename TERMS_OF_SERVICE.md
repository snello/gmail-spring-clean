## 2. Description of Service

Gmail Spring Clean is a Chrome extension that scans your Gmail inbox to identify sender domains by message volume. It allows you to:

- View which domains send you the most email.
- Trash all unlabelled messages from a specific domain.
- Purge old promotional emails.
- Ignore domains from future scans.
- Search Gmail for messages from a specific domain.
